About Us

Precision Dentistry, Compassionate Care

Located in the heart of Olarikkara, Thrissur, DENTCRAFT Multispeciality Dental Clinic is built on a foundation of clinical excellence and patient-centred care. Our team of specialist dentists work across all disciplines to provide seamless, comprehensive treatment under one roof.

We combine advanced diagnostic technology with evidence-based treatment protocols — ensuring every patient receives accurate diagnosis, clear explanations, and the most appropriate course of treatment.

Modern Technology

State-of-the-art equipment for accurate diagnosis and precision treatment.

Strict Hygiene

International sterilisation standards maintained throughout the clinic.

Child-Friendly

Patient, gentle approach to make young patients feel at ease.

Clear Communication

Every step explained in detail so you always understand your care.

What We Offer

Our Services

From preventive care to advanced cosmetic procedures — comprehensive dental services delivered by specialists.

Teeth Whitening

Professional in-clinic whitening treatments for a brighter, more confident smile.

Veneers & Crowns

Custom-crafted porcelain veneers and crowns to restore and enhance tooth aesthetics.

Bonding

Tooth-coloured composite bonding to repair chips, cracks and minor imperfections.

Teeth Reshaping

Subtle contouring procedures to improve shape, length and surface of teeth.

Cosmetic Procedures

Comprehensive smile makeover treatments tailored to your individual goals.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements with titanium implant technology.

Fillings & Sealants

Tooth-coloured composite fillings and protective sealants for lasting restoration.

Dentures & Bridges

Removable and fixed prosthetic solutions to restore full dental function.

Root Canals

Precise endodontic treatment to save damaged or infected teeth comfortably.

Clear Aligners / Invisalign

Certified Invisalign provider — discreet, removable orthodontic correction.

Orthodontist

Full orthodontic assessment and treatment planning for children and adults.

Oral Surgery

Minor and complex oral surgical procedures performed under appropriate anaesthesia.

Extractions

Safe, gentle tooth extractions including impacted wisdom teeth removal.

Dental Checkups

Thorough clinical examinations and radiographic assessments for early detection.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar and surface stains.

Mouth Guards

Custom-fitted guards for bruxism protection and sports safety.
